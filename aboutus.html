HOW WE
STARTED

Established in 2023 in Angeles City, Pampanga, Rockwell Music was founded with a clear vision to provide musicians with access to quality guitars that combine reliability, craftsmanship, and exceptional sound. From the beginning, our goal has been to create a trusted destination where guitarists, whether first-time players or seasoned performers, can explore a carefully curated selection from respected brands. Each instrument is selected with precision to ensure it meets the standards of playability and performance that every musician deserves.

To keep our collection dynamic and relevant, we receive a new batch of guitars each week, giving customers the opportunity to discover fresh options and the latest arrivals on a regular basis. Although Rockwell Music is a young name in the industry, we remain committed to professionalism, consistency, and service excellence, always grounded in our beginnings in Pampanga.
